Switcher

  • Genre: 2D Side Scroller
  • Engine: Game Maker Studio 2
  • Language: GML
  • Team size: 1
  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Role: All

The first game I ever made!

The idea behind the game was a simple 2D Side Scroller where you bounce back and forth trying to get through the next hole and you also have to make sure your character is the right shape if not you die!

The game ended up being really really addictive and fun and I really want to return to it or re make it from scratch in a different engine at some point.

I feel like where I left the game off it was a Demo, so playable! but... lacking in content and plenty of bugs!
