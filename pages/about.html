About Me

Nathaniel Brandal

My name is Nathaniel Brandal. I am a Game Developer currently located in Norway. My passion is making games and to explore every creative corner of the universe. I never say no to work for things I believe in and could never say no to the oppertunity to learn something new.

Ever since I got access to my first computer I was browsing the internet like crazy and exploring every aspect of it. Then I wanted to understand it, learn how to make things for myself. It took a few years before I had the oppertunity to start learning and expanding my knowledge. But eventually I did and it was the best decision I have ever made, I finally got to start turning my creative ideas in to a reality.

My past: What is important to know about me is my illness. As I am diagnosed with Chronic Fatigue which makes it very hard for me to retain my energy. Which has taught me how to portion my energy out over the day/week/month. Even though this is a negative thing, I still take great pride in being able to do the things I do and not letting my illness hinder me from doing what I want.

My Education:

Buskerud Folkehøgskole, Fiskum. What I learned most from spending a year at this school was teamwork and responsibility. We spent the year learning about game design and working togheter as a group.

My Work Experience:

Bygdeservice, Bjørkelangen. Through this job I learned how to work effectively in a team and being efficient at solving problems that occur at work.
